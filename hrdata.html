HR Attendance Data Analysis

This project provides an in-depth analysis of employee attendance data, focusing on key metrics such as presence, work from home (WFH) percentages, and sick leave (SL) percentages using Power BI. Key insights include:

  • Attendance Overview: Detailed attendance records and trends.
  • WFH Trends: Percentage of WFH days relative to working days.
  • Sick Leave Trends: Analysis of sick leave trends.
  • Monthly Breakdown: Insights into attendance patterns by month.
  • Employee Presence: Detailed visualizations of employee presence data.

Visualizations include charts, graphs, and tables to provide a comprehensive view of the data and facilitate better decision-making.

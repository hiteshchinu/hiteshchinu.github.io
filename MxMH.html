Exploratory Data Analysis of Music and Mental Health Survey

This project involves analyzing the MxMH survey dataset to explore correlations between music preferences and self-reported mental health. The key aspects include:

  • Survey Analysis: Investigating how different music tastes relate to mental health status.
  • Data Cleaning: Handling missing values and preparing the dataset for analysis.
  • Visualization: Creating plots and charts to visualize trends and insights.
  • Insights: Identifying potential relationships and making recommendations based on the data.

The analysis includes detailed visualizations and statistical summaries to provide a comprehensive view of the data.
